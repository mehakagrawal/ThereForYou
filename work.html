Our Work

Peer Support Group Meetings

Peer support group meetings overseen by trained peer coordinators foster members to share their problems and experiences.
It aims to develop a space which promotes emotional and mental well being of each member in a confidential, accessible and an inclusive environment.


Mental health matters, take charge of your mental health from this New Year 2021?

What?


Peer-facilitated support groups consists (6-10 students per group) that are cost-free and confidential. Members would not know about each other's identity, and even the coordinator will not be aware of it. It is just the place where you can speak your heart out with absolutely no fear of being judged!

When/Where?


Group meeting are held on Saturday and Sunday, in 1-hour time slots for 4-8 pm each day. All the meetings will be held virtually. Join us by signing up above!


Mental Health Awareness Events

In addition to the peer support group, mental awareness and well being events will be organized bi-weekly or weekly on campus and online. These events will include:-
1. Self care Sunday events.
2. Virtual game night- these sessions will focus on improving mental strength with the use of some brain games.
3. Mental health awareness and educational sessions.
