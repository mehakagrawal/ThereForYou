The PSG, We're Here to Hear!

"Healing takes time, and asking for help is a courageous step" -Mariska Hargitay

2020 has not been that good a year for all of us, and we all need to speak our hearts out as we human beings are obvious to behave as social animals. We all need to vent our frustrations, failures, anxiety-provoking thoughts, and insecurities out.
There For You brings to all of you a golden opportunity in the form of a Peer Support Group - managed and organized by highly empathetic future therapists, psychology students.
It is just what we need in this 'pandemic world'!
It offers an array of benefits, and here are some to bring to your notice :

1. 100% Confidentiality with no fear of judgment
Confiding in someone about your deepest and darkest feelings and emotions is hard and arduous. After bottling up patience and worrisome feelings, we feel comfortable in opening up. To make things simpler, we have brought out a feature of anonymity. By joining our support group, your identity will be kept confidential. Members would not know about each other's identity, and even the coordinator will not be aware of it. It is just the place where you can speak your heart out with absolutely no fear of being judged!

2. Free of cost
Sometimes seeking support and help comes with a cost, and it is not possible for everyone to afford the stipulated support. We are fully aware of this possibility, and therefore, our peer support group is open to everyone irrespective of their economic background. There is no entry or exit charge. Isn't that just fabulous?

3. Empathetic and Authentic support system
The peer group has highly efficient, driven, and selfless supporters who will make sure that you feel comfortable. You will never be forced to open up unless you want to, by yourself. Furthermore, we understand and preach empathy which means the ability to understand and share the feelings of other person as opposed to sympathy which means feeling compassion and kindness towards others. By having this empathy, our supporters would be able to relate and understand you better.

4. Promotion of Mental, Emotional and Psychological well-being
According to the World Health Organization, good health is the state of complete physical, mental, emotional, and psychological well-being. By being a part of our support group, you can be more emotionally balanced than ever and gradually evolve better coping mechanisms and cognitive structures. Ultimately, this catharsis will lead to your emotional healing.

If you wish to know more about our peer support group, don't hesitate to reach out.
Always remember that you do not need to suffer alone because no one deserves that.
We are here for you, and we have got you.
