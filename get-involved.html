Help as a Health Worker

Are you a psychologist, psychiatrist, therapist, psychiatric counselor, social worker, or anyone else involved in the mental health world?
We can use your help! Health Workers could be:-

  • Present worldwide, Individual volunteers or volunteers as part of a larger organization.
  • Ready to lend their advice and insights into mental health and general wellbeing of an individual for us including our branches.
  • Volunteering can be done in a way of leading workshops, giving speeches, training of student coordinators or being available as a volunteer expert consultant in mental health issues, especially for any updates and healthcare plans.

Start Our Branch at Your Campus

There for you Branches are our catalyst for expanding the mission of destigmatize mental illness and for increasing access to affordable, quality treatment.
We follow a co-op approach where branches work together with HQ to advocate for mental health.
That’s why all TFY branches are officially named as There for you + {College Name}. But this isn't a hierarchical system. We want you to give you the power and autonomy to make change that makes the most sense for your community.

Branches will -

  • Receive tailored, individual mentoring, resources, and support from HQ to carry out their projects and mission effectively.
  • Can comprise of n number of people, as the branch head desires to, as long as the overall mission of Tfy is not diluted due to difficult logistics and the group size.
  • It would enable individuals to participate in anonymous peer support meetings.
  • They will receive TFY services provided by health worker volunteers and headquarters, including workshops for peers on mental health.
